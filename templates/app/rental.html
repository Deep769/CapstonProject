Mission Statement

It is our mission is to set the standard of high performance and manage residential and commercial properties for the success of our clients. In doing this we are ensuring all our residents have the best quality, most comforting and affordable living experience possible. We strive to listen to customers who represent the marketplace. The key to our growth and prosperity is a direct result of our exceptional management team and together we strive for excellence and to ensure the success of our client, the well-being of our properties and the future of Columbus Rentals LLC.

History

Multifamily real estate is our passion. We love having the opportunity to create a true sense of community while building careers and providing a means for personal wealth through investment.Columbus Rentals LLC is a locally owned business that has specialized in residential and commercial real estate & development since 1980. We serve urban communities of Central Ohio with an eye toward historical preservation as well as adding some modern rentals. Our goal is to provide our clients with new innovative designs that accommodate their needs, budgets, and lifestyles while maintaining whatever made that specific property special in the first place. We make it a priority to provide quality workmanship, professionalism, integrity, and attention-to detail on every project.
